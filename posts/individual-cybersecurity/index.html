password management

pw manager, use different, random passwords for everything

Backups (incl. offsite server)

store important data (especially the pw database) on backup drives. setup a small offsite server (like a raspberry pi with a harddrive attached) as an offsite backup, so in case of fire or something that destroys your data at home you dont lose all your passwords / accounts also print out your pw db for exmample from time to time. maybe loosely encrypt the passwords in some way though like, print out every password but apply a casar or rotary shift to them. should look exactly the same, bcause they should be cryptic and saves you from someone finding the documents and knowing everything.

Avoidance of spyware (incl. windows, whatsapp etc.)

Switch to privacy respecting, open-source software. And dont say I have nothing to hide as an arugment against switching. I mean, would you say you are against freedom of speech and freedom of the press just because you don’t want to participate in politics yourself? I know that this analogy doesnt 100% hold up but IMO its a nice visualization.

If you are crazy

Use Libreboot. CPU manufacturers install backdoors in your bootloader. Switch to Custom Phone ROM, with locked bootloader. At best use a phone with hardware killswitches for camera, microphone, gps etc
